     BEST PRACTICES

???????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????????
?  ? DO:      ?
?  • Keep page styles in their respective CSS files        ?
?  • Use design system variables for colors     ?
?  • Follow naming conventions       ?
?  • Include responsive styles in same file?
?  • Add comments for complex styles     ?
?  • Test on multiple screen sizes    ?
?      ?
?  ? DON'T:       ?
?  • Add page-specific styles to site.css   ?
?  • Use inline styles in Razor pages?
?  • Use !important unnecessarily          ?
?  • Hardcode colors (use variables)    ?
?  • Create deeply nested selectors       ?
?• Forget mobile responsiveness           ?
